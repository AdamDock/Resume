About Me

I am a Software Engineer within the React framework. My passion for technology, coupled with a lifelong enthusiasm for mathematics and data analysis, has driven my career to programming.

My interest in the intersection of data analytics, finance, and sports was sparked by two influential sources: "Moneyball," a groundbreaking baseball analytics book, and Ray Dalio, an early innovator in big data and fintech within the investment industry. These interests motivated me to explore programming as a means to enhance efficiency and innovation in these domains.

I embarked on my coding journey by taking on a Python project as an extracurricular activity in 7th grade. Since then, I have experimented with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C, C++, and SQL, while completing my associate's degree in computer science. I believe that the true mastery of knowledge comes from the practical application of principles, and I am eager to continue evolving as a programmer.
